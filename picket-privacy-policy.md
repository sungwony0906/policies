# Picket — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 12, 2026_

Picket does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data or browsing data. Period.

- **No network requests.** The extension contains no networking code. Nothing ever leaves your device.
- **What it reads:** when you click "Extract", Picket reads the hyperlinks (URL and anchor text) of the page(s) you point it at, displays them to you, and discards them when the popup closes.
- **What it stores:** only your filter preferences (e.g. "dedupe on"), saved locally via Chrome's `storage.local` on your own device.
- **No analytics, no trackers, no accounts, no remote code.**

The source repository's test suite includes an automated check asserting the extension makes zero external network requests.
