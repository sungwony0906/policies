# Trellis — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 12, 2026_

Trellis does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. Everything Trellis does happens on your device.

- **No network requests.** The extension contains no networking code; our automated test suite asserts zero external network requests.
- **Conversation content** is read from claude.ai pages you visit solely to provide search and export, and is stored only in your browser's local IndexedDB. It never leaves your device. You can wipe the index anytime in Settings.
- **Folders, prompts and preferences** are stored locally via Chrome's `storage.local`.
- **No analytics, no trackers, no accounts, no remote code.**
- Trellis is an independent project, not affiliated with Anthropic. It interacts only with your own claude.ai session, in your own browser, at your direction.
